GMATWiz · visual identity

The instrument, not the hype machine.

GMATWiz treats competence the way a good lab treats a measurement: with a reading, a margin of error, and the discipline to say “not yet” when the data is thin. The interface is built to feel like a precise, calm instrument.

The signature element — “The Measure”

Prototype screens

01 Onboarding & diagnostic Leads with the gauge abstaining. Exam date, weekly time, a framed 20-min pretest. 02 Today One clear next action. Bypassable daily rhythm, no streaks, quiet progress. 03 Lesson loop I do · we do · you do. The explanation is sealed until you attempt. 04 Review session Focus mode: chrome shrinks to a thin rule. Spaced recall, calm grading. 05 Error log Required review, framed kindly. One-tap “why,” then scheduled re-practice. 06 Dashboard Memory, Performance, Readiness — three separate readings, two abstaining.
